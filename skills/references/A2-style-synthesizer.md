# A2: Style Synthesizer

Combine multiple A1 analyses into a comprehensive, actionable style guide.

## Instructions

Based on the video analyses provided, create:

### 1. THE STYLE DNA
- **Voice Signature:** Consistent tone, energy, personality across all videos
- **Core Philosophy:** Underlying belief about selling that drives this style
- **Authenticity Formula:** What makes this creator feel trustworthy

### 2. THE THREE VIDEO ARCHETYPES
For each pattern identified:
- **Archetype Name:** [Descriptive name]
- **When to Use:** [Best product types/situations]
- **Structure Template:** [Step-by-step format]
- **Example Hooks:** [3-5 hooks that fit this archetype]

### 3. UNIVERSAL PATTERNS
- **Opening Moves:** Hook formulas that work across all videos
- **Transition Phrases:** How they move from problem to solution
- **Proof Presentation:** How they show evidence without bragging
- **CTA Style:** Their approach to asking for action

### 4. PSYCHOLOGICAL TOOLKIT
- **Pain Point Articulation:** How they describe problems
- **Trust Building Techniques:** What creates credibility
- **Urgency Creation:** How they motivate action
- **Objection Handling:** How they preempt doubts

### 5. LANGUAGE PATTERNS
- **Signature Phrases:** Recurring words/expressions
- **Rhythm & Pacing:** Sentence length patterns
- **Authenticity Markers:** What makes it feel unscripted
- **Avoid List:** What this style never does

### 6. VISUAL/DELIVERY GUIDE
- **Energy Spectrum:** When high vs. low energy
- **Camera Techniques:** Preferred visual approaches
- **Show vs. Tell Ratio:** How much demonstration vs. talking

### 7. ADAPTATION FRAMEWORK
How to apply this style to ANY product:
- Step 1: [...]
- Step 2: [...]
- Step 3: [...]

## Input Format

```
Please paste all the video breakdowns from Step A1:

[BREAKDOWN 1]
[BREAKDOWN 2]
[BREAKDOWN 3]
```
