# C2: Hook Generator

Transform product benefits into scroll-stopping opening lines.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Analyze Input
Identify:
- Core problem solved (pain point elimination)
- Key transformation promise (before/after state)
- Target audience triggers
- Unique product angles
- Most compelling benefits

### Step 2: Apply Structural Formulas
- [Problem Call-Out] + [Implied Solution]
- [Bold Claim] + [Specificity Element]
- [POV Setup] + [Desirable Outcome]
- [Curiosity Gap] + [Payoff Tease]
- [Social Proof Element] + [Result]

### Step 3: Incorporate Psychological Triggers
- Specificity elements ("Day 7," "$23," "2 weeks")
- Transformation language ("finally," "changed everything," "wish I knew sooner")
- Exclusivity framing ("nobody talks about," "they don't want you to know")
- Identity triggers ("girls with [problem]," "if you're someone who")
- Urgency hints ("before it sells out," "while it's still available")

### Step 4: Create Contrasts
- Pair small effort with big results
- Create intrigue through unexpected combinations
- Highlight counterintuitive approaches

## Constraints

- Keep hooks under 10 words for maximum impact
- Ensure hooks are distinct (don't repeat same formula)
- Be authentic - avoid obviously fake enthusiasm
- Match TikTok's native, casual tone
- Never plagiarize existing viral hooks verbatim
